Process

How I Work

I am currently enrolled in SEG3125 – Analysis and Design of User Interfaces at the University of Ottawa, where I am learning and applying the following design principles in each project:

01

Understand & Research

I start by understanding the users and their needs before touching a single line of code or wireframe. Good design is invisible — it starts with listening.

02

Apply Visual Communication Principles

Drawing from course material (NN/g), I structure layouts using Scale, Visual Hierarchy, Balance, and Contrast to guide the user's eye naturally through the interface.

03

Build & Iterate

I prototype using Bootstrap and vanilla JS, deploy on Vercel, and iterate based on real feedback — applying the same iterative process I've learned through both coursework and personal projects.

04

Reflect & Document

Every design decision is intentional and documented — from colour palette rationale to typography choices — so others can understand and build on my work.
